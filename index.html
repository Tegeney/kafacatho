School News & Events

Annual Science Fair 2025

April 15, 2025

Join us for an exciting day of experiments and discoveries at our Annual Science Fair. Students will showcase innovative projects in biology, chemistry, and physics. The event will feature special guest judges from Addis Ababa University's Science Department.

Graduation Ceremony

June 20, 2025

Celebrating our Grade 8 stars as they complete their primary education journey. The ceremony will take place in the school courtyard at 10:00 AM, followed by a cultural performance and lunch reception. All parents and community members are welcome.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

May 5-7, 2025

Schedule your appointment to discuss your child's progress and achievements. Meetings will be held from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM each day. Please sign up at the school office or contact your child's homeroom teacher to reserve your time slot.
